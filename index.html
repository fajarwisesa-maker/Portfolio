Who Am I?

I am a motivated and adaptable Informatics student at President University with a growing focus on Data Science, data-driven problem solving, and analytical thinking. I am deeply interested in how data can be transformed into insights, predictions, and decisions that create real-world impact, especially through statistics, machine learning, and practical data analysis.

I am driven by curiosity and consistency. I enjoy breaking down complex problems, exploring patterns in data, and iterating on solutions through experimentation and evaluation. Rather than just building models, I focus on understanding the why behind the data and the impact behind the results.

Beyond technical skills, I bring discipline, adaptability, and a strong interest in applying data science to meaningful, real-world use cases.
